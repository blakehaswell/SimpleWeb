Web Design Process

Research → Design → Build → Launch

Research

The first part of our process is research. Before we can propose any solutions, we need to understand your company and the problem we’re there to solve.

At the stage we conduct many different types of research including stakeholder and customer interviews, gathering technical requirements, analysing any existing metrics, reviewing your existing online and offline marketing presence, and more.

This research gives us the information we need to come up with solutions that meet your needs and budget.
